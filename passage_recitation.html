How to fill this section

  • Click + Add Student to add a new student entry. You can add up to 10 students.
  • Click Delete Student to remove a student you have added.
  • Fill in First Name, Gender and Standard before uploading the video — upload will stay disabled until these are filled.
  • You must upload a video for every student before continuing to the next page.
  • Maximum upload size is 1 GB per video.
  • Do NOT refresh this page while filling the form — your current data and uploads will be lost.
